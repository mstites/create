Who said engineers had to be all logic and no liminality?

I sometimes find it necessary to escape the technical swamp and dart into the artistic caverns of my brain. Create is a porthole into those realms. The site features works which draw heavily on my lived experiences as a lesbian, poly, non-binary, trans woman.

There’s a zines page which features collections of my poetry, short essays, and journals from different stages of my trans experiences.

In the future, I hope to share more pieces focused on trans positivity, allyship, and intersectionality. My creations — and by extension, this site — follow my transition. As I find ways to cope with my trauma, I feel that my work will shift accordingly.
