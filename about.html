About Me

This site was created by lesbian, poly, non-binary, trans woman Maeve Stites. She created this site to display and document her creative side projects. Create is an ongoing project she plans to continue updating for the foreseeable future.

She is an Electrical and Computer Engineering major at Olin College of Engineering with special interests in robotics, programming, and engineering for social good. In addition to engineering, she has a passion for social justice and diversity, equity, and inclusion work.

Outside of her work, she enjoys a variety of hobbies including writing zines, Linux shenanigans, custom computer keyboards, fashion, and rugby.
