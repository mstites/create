Resources for transgender or questioning people. This is a work in progress. Please feel free to reach out with any suggestions or feedback.

Am I trans?

I am still looking for more resources in this area! But in short, there is no one who can approve you as being trans. It is perfectly okay to explore pronouns/names/expression or whatever you want without any strings or labels attached.

It is extremely normal for transgender people to take time to explore their identities. I know I am still figuring things out! Oh, and you don't need dysphoria to be trans! There is no one right way to be transgender.
