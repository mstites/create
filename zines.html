Zines and Writing

A small sample of the zines I have written, mostly focusing on my experiences with gender discrimination and dysphoria. I plan on creating new updated poetry zines in the near future, as well as compiling some of my other writings into zine format.

Please feel free to make copies and distribute, as long as accredation is given. Please reach out if interested in any sort of collaborative work or suggestions.
